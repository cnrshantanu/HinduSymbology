How to use the App

Click on the View Gods to see the list of Gods featured in this App.

You have the option of viewing the list of Gods by Classification or Alphabetically.

At the top of the screen you will see 3 options:

The Hindu God Symbology App was created to help people better understand the symbolic significance of the various Hindu Deities, and the meaning of the symbols they hold in their hands, the ornaments that adorn their bodies, their posture, and associated colors.

Markers

When you select Markers, blinking white circles appear over the God image. Click on any of the circle and a description of that particular symbol is shown

Symbology

Click on Symbology to discover God information, including Puranic stories, mythology, legends, historical facts, associated festivals.

Summary

Click on Summary to discover the symbolic meaning of the God derived from Creation, Cosmology, Astrology, Nature, Phycology, Spiritual and Personal Development.
