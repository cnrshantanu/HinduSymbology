Krishna

Peacock Feather: Sign of beauty, knowledge and observation.

Flower Garland: Natural essence of life and devotion.

Flute: Tuning of bodily energies (chakras).

Krishna: Represents supreme consciousness in the earthly realm.

Supported by Krishna: Represents obtaining strength through devotion

Radha: Represents love and devotion for God.

Yellow Clothes: Represents the earth realm.

Jewelry: Wealth follows once you seek for the higher Truth

Crown: Represents supremacy and authority.

Mark on Forehead: Sattva or serenity.

Placing Right Foot Towards Left: Represents that material bliss can be found within spiritual world.

Krishna with Cow: Protector of the innocent.

Radha & Krishna: Interdependence between divine and devotee.
