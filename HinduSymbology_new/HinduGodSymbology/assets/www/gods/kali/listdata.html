Kali

Sword: Represents cutting through ignorance and destroying false consciousness.

Trident: Three Human qualities. Satwa- purity of thought; Rajas- Energy of Desires; Tamas- Inertia.

White Teeth: Symbol of purity.

Hair Let Open: Representing Demand of acknowledgement.

Male Head Garland: 52 heads signifying 52 letter in Sanskrit and hence knowledge.

Belt of Arms: Freedom from cycle of Karma (action)

Black Complexion: Comprehensive nature (all colors disappear into black).

Red Tongue: Represents consuming all things and tasting what society regards as forbidden.

Sleeping Shiva: Represents without Shakti (Power), Shiva is inert (like a corpse, shava)

Male Head: Represent destruction of ego in ignorance.

Nudity: Free from all covering of illusions and trappings of ignorance.

Cremation Ground: Symbolizes inner burning away of attachment, anger, lust, greed, jealousy etc.
