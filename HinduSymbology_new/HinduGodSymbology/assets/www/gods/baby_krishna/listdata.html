Baby Krishna

Mark on Forehead: Sattva or serenity.

Upper Body: Represents a person’s spiritual nature.

Sucking the Right Toe: Co-dependence of material and spiritual existence.

Lower Body: Represents a person’s materialistic nature.

Flute: Tuning of bodily energies (chakras).

Peacock Feather: Represents beauty and divinity which is the center of attraction.

Blue Colored Skin: Represents infinite attributes.

Banyan leaf: Represents the nature of divinity to be timeless.

Water: Represents the infinite universe.
