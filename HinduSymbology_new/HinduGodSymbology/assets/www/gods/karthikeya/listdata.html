Karthikeya

Crown: Supreme authority.

Spear: Far reaching protection and development of practical skills.

Open Hand: Blessing for protection.

Peacock: Represents vanity and desires. To attain spiritual bliss, one must tame the peacock in them.

The Snake: Expression of human ego.

Banana Tree: Abundance and fertility.

Boyish Appearance: Innocent and pure intentions.

Flower Garland: Represents devotion.

Yellow Clothes: Represents the earthly realm.

Burning Lamp: Represents pure knowledge illuminating ignorance.
