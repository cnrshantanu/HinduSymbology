Crown: Supreme authority over knowledge.

Back Right Hand: (Ahankara) represents ego.

Front Right Hand: Represents mind (Buddhi).

The Four Arms: Represents omnipresence and omnipotence.

White: Embodiment of pure knowledge.

Swan (Hansa): Represents discriminating intellect (ability to make good choices)

Veena: Represents tuning of mind and intellect to live in perfect harmony.

Back Left Hand: (Chitta) represents conditioned consciousness.

Front Left Hand: Represents intellect (Buddhi).

Book: Represents acquiring knowledge for good.

Lotus: Firm establishment in the evolving consciousness.

Waiting Peacock: Represents overcoming unpredictability and fickleness of mind to acquire true knowledge.

Pearl Necklace: Represents concentration meditation, and contemplation to overcome ego.

Natural Setting: Perfect ambience for literary pursuits.

Right Side of Body: Activities of mind and intellect.

Glow Behind Head: Aura of knowledge and purity.

Seated on a Rock: Pure intellect.

River: Signifies free flowing form of knowledge.

Left Side of Body: Activities of heart.
