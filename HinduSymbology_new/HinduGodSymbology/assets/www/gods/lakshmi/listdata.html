Lakshmi

Crown: Prosperity and superiority.

Back Right Hand: Represents liberation from birth and death (Moksha).

Front Right Hand: Represents wealth (Artha).

Falling Golden Coins: Represents wealth and prosperity through right actions.

Lotus: True wealth remains unchanged regardless of external surroundings.

Back Right Hand: (Dharma) represents righteousness or purpose of life.

Front Left Hand: (Kama) represents genuine desires.

Flower Garland: Devotion.

Red Saree & Gold Border: Creative activity & prosperity

2 Elephants: Name and fame associated with worldly wealth which should not be one’s sole purpose in life.

Golden Glow: Signifies aura of supreme wealth.

Lotus in Right Hand: Performing duties to achieve moksha.

Plate of Wealth: Signifies accumulation of wealth.

Lotus in left hand: Performing duties to achieve dharma.

Elephants Pouring Water: Represents action governed by wisdom and purity to achieve one’s dharma (life purpose) leading to material and spiritual prosperity.

Plate of Fruits: Sattvik or pure interests.

Owl: Represents Alakshmi, elder twin of misfortune which signifies balance in life.
