Ganesh

Elephants Head: Represents wisdom, understanding and intellect required to attain perfection in life.

Open Hand: Represents divine blessings and protection.

Tip of the trunk: Represents discriminatory intellect (making careful choices).

Right over the Left Leg: Represents using knowledge and reason to overcome emotions for good life.

Wide Mouth: Represents desire to enjoy life in the world.

Small Eyes: Represents ability to focus on one’s objective.

Big ears: Represents capacity to listen to others.

Elephants Trunk:  Represents the human mind’s ability to perform wide range of task through strength and determination.

Tusks: Represents that wisdom (full tusk) is required to master emotional (broken tusk) imperfection.

Right Side of Body:   Symbolizes reason and knowledge.

Left Side of Body:  Symbolizes emotions.

Pot Belly: Represents the capacity of an individual to face all pleasant and unpleasant experiences.

Crown: Think big and wisely.

Pot of Gems:  Spiritual enlightenment.

Axe: Represents cutting worldly attachments.

Open Palm: Protection and blessings.

Assortment of Food: Material wealth, power and prosperity.

Rope: To pull towards your goal.

Lotus plume: Represents attaining higher consciousness (spiritual perfection)

Modak (Rice Ball): Rewards for the spiritual practice.

Yellow & Red Dhoti: Yellow symbolizes purity, peace and truthfulness. Red symbolizes action.

Rat: Represents one must master ego (mouse represents ego that can nibble away at ones good qualities)
