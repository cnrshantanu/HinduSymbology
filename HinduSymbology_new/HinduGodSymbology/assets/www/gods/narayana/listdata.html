Narayana

Flower Garland: Natural essence of life.

Conch: Represents the mystic sound of Aum.

Peacock Feather: A myriad of colors showing all moods of life.

Ocean: The still cosmic ocean which concurs with the universe.

Lotus: Represents purity and beauty of evolved consciousness.

Brahma Emerges from the Navel: The source of creation.

Lotus: Emerging from naval represents consciousness.

Sudarshan Chakra: Represents destruction of darkness (ignorance).

Yellow Dhoti: Represent presence of infinite truth on the earth.

Lakshmi: Goddess of wealth to maintain the world.

Crown: Represents Lordship of the entire universe.

Mark on Forehead: Symbolic of highest form of Satva or serenity.

Coiled Serpent: Represents stillness & potential.

Multiple Snake Heads: Commonly only 5 heads representing the 5 primordial states of matter (matter, fire, air wind and ether).

Mace: Power of knowledge and destruction of ignorance.
