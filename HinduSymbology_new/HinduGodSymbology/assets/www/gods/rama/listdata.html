Rama

Bow & Arrows: Represents that we have the potential to destroy negative desires protect righteousness.

Flower Garland: Natural essence of divinity

Lakshmana: Symbolizes personal sacrifice for the right cause.

Hanumana: Symbolizes the qualities of an ideal devotee (See hanumana).

Rama: Perfect son, devoted brother, true husband, trusted friend, noble adversary and ideal king which is the perfect example of dharma.

Yellow clothes: Represents divine relationship to earthly realm (same as Vishnu)

Sita: Represents ideal daughter, wife, mother and queen.
