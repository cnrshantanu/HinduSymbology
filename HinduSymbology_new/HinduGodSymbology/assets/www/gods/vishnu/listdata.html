Vishnu

Peacock Feather: Colors of life.

Sudarshan Chakra: Destruction of evil.

Crown: Supreme power and authority.

Ear Rings: Dual nature of creation.

Mace: Power to sustain creation.

Lotus Plume: Represents perfection and beauty.

Standing on Lotus: Achievement of highest state of consciousness.

Yellow & Blue: Represent presence of infinite truth on the earth.

Blue Colored Skin: Infinite attributes.

Jewelry: Wealth follows once you seek for the higher truth.

Flower Garland: Represents beauty and natural essence of life and devotion.

Golden Glow: Represents self realization and evolved conscience.

Multiple Snake Heads: Represents mastery over innumerable poisonous desires of mind.

Front Right Hand: Giving Blessings.

Two Front Arms: Represents activity in the physical world.

Right Part of the Body: Represents activities of the mind and intellect.

Yellow Dhoti (Clothes): Represents earthly existence and upholding righteousness.

Conch: Represents divine communication to people to live with kindness and compassion.

Blue Sky: Represents God is present in the infinite universe.

Two Back Arms: Represents activity in the spiritual world.

Left Part of the Body: Represents activities of the heart (love, kindness and compassion).
