Brahma

Crown: Authority over creation.

The Four Heads: Representation of the four vedas and knowledge.

Back Right Hand: Mind.

Front Right Hand: Giving blessings to overcome ego.

Lotus: Beauty and essence of consciousness.

Scriptures: Represents cultivation of mind through knowledge.

Flower Garland: Represents beauty and natural essence of life and devotion.

Left Hand: Intellect.

Pot of Water: Cosmic energy from which existence emerges.

Prayer Beads: Cyclic nature of creation.

Off-White Dhoti: Represents dual nature of creation, happiness-unhappiness etc.

Golden Glow: Represents energy needed for the constant process of creation.

Front Left Hand: Conditioned consciousness.

White Beard: Wisdom.
